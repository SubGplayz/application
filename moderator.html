Moderation Application

Welcome to our server's staff application form for moderation.
As a moderator you will assist players with any concerns, including answering questions and punishing rule breakers, alongside interacting with the community and staff team.
Before applying, make sure to do the following:

  • Read over the Server's rules, which can be found in the discord server.
  • Fill out this application with as much detail as possible.
  • Be completely honest in your application.


You will receive a response to your application within a day, where you will be informed if you have been invited to an interview, or denied.
Please be patient while waiting for your application to be answered. If you ask any staff to review your application, it will be denied.

You must be at least 13 years old to apply to join our staff.
